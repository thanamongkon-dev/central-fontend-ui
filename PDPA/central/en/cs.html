Customer Service and Reception Privacy Policy

Last Update 27 May 2020

This Customer Privacy Policy (this “ Privacy Policy ”) describes how Central Department Store Ltd., (referred to as “ Company “ we ”, “ our ” or “ us ”) collect, use, or disclose your personal data in relation to your contact of our customer service.

For the purpose of this Privacy Policy, “ Personal Data ” means “any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

1. What Personal Data Do We Collect?

We may collect or receive the following types of personal data directly from you:

Contact Information such as name, address, phone number, mobile number, email address, national ID number, etc.

Membership Information such as details of your membership account (e.g. The 1 card), membership type, and date of joining, etc.

Identification Information such as gender, nationality, date of birth, occupation, education, marital status, national ID number, passport number, driver's license number, vehicle registration number, recipient's name and phone number, signature, photo, or similar identification documents, etc.

Interests such as information indicating your interests, etc.

Financial Information such as bank account, payment details and history, credit/debit card information, etc.

Website Usage and Communication Information such as IP address, location data, online search history, browser type and version, time zone setting, operating system and platform, and other technologies on the devices you use to access our website, etc.

Transaction and Service Usage Information such as your purchases or orders, delivery information, complaints and claims, and other contact information, etc.

Marketing and Communication Information such as how you learned about us, your preferences for receiving marketing information from us, and your preferred communication methods, etc.

2. Why Do We Collect, Use, or Disclose Your Personal Data?

We collect, use, and disclose your personal data for the following purposes:

1). To provide you with our full range of services, to fulfill our contractual obligations with you, or to take necessary steps prior to entering into such contracts.

2). To handle inquiries, complaints, and product warranties, and to provide assistance regarding products and technical support.

3). To process refunds, returns, exchanges, claims, compensation, payments, gift registration, point redemption, lost and found services, rentals, loans, and warranty notifications for our products or services.

4). To register you as a new customer or for our newsletter, or to provide account services.

5). To manage and develop our relationship with you, such as customer satisfaction surveys and customer relations marketing management.

6). To identify you and respond to your requests.

7). To maintain the security and reliability of our business operations.

8). To improve, develop, and maintain the quality of our services.

9). To manage and prevent loss of property and premises.

10). For compliance with our terms and conditions.

11). To detect and prevent misconduct within our premises.

12). To monitor situations.

13). To prevent and report criminal offenses in our stores, including maintaining the security and ethics of our business.

14). To prevent or mitigate harm to your life, body, and health.

15). For the public benefit in protecting other customers in our premises.

16). To comply with legal obligations between us and/or cooperate with courts, regulators, government agencies, and law enforcement authorities for the exercise of state powers applicable to us.

17). To exercise or protect our legal rights as necessary and permitted by law, such as detecting, preventing, and responding to fraud claims, intellectual property infringement, or non-compliance with laws.

If we are legally required to collect, use, and disclose your personal data or to fulfill our contract with you, and you cannot provide your personal data, we may not be able to provide our services to you or fully perform our contract with you (e.g. customer service or responding to your requests at our reception desk).

3. How Long Do We Retain Your Personal Data?

We will retain your personal data for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected. However, we may retain your personal data for a longer period if required to comply with applicable laws.

4. Who May We Disclose or Transfer Your Personal Data To?

We may disclose your personal data to other companies in our group, affiliates, subsidiaries, business partners, service providers, legal advisors, auditors, courts, regulators, government agencies, and law enforcement authorities. You can view our group company list here.

5. Transfer of Your Personal Data Abroad

Your personal data may be transferred to other countries, which may or may not have the same personal data protection standards as Thailand. However, we will ensure that any transfer of your personal data is carried out in accordance with applicable laws before proceeding.

6. What Rights Do You Have Regarding Your Personal Data?

Subject to the provisions and exceptions under applicable laws, you may have the right to request access and/or obtain a copy, transfer, correct, delete, destroy, or anonymize certain types of your personal data we hold, suspend and/or object to certain activities involving your personal data. If we have acted based on your consent, you may withdraw your consent, but we may not be able to provide our services to you in full. You may also request us to disclose how we obtained your personal data without your consent and file a complaint with the relevant authority under applicable law.
