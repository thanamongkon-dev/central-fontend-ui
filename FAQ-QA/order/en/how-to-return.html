How to return

1. Return at a Store Branch

  • You can return the product along with the receipt containing the order number at the Click & Collect service point at your preferred branch.
  • You can check the location of the service points by scanning the QR code below.

2. Pickup Return Service

  • Contact Customer Service through one of the following three channels (Operating hours: Monday – Sunday, 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM):

Phone:

1425 press 1 (Available daily from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM)

Email:

contactcenter@central.co.th

Facebook Inbox:

https://www.facebook.com/CentralOnlineShopping/
  • Customer Service will inform you of the pickup date along with the return preparation guidelines.
  • You will receive an SMS with the tracking number before the scheduled pickup date.
  • On the pickup day, provide the details from the SMS and hand over the product to the staff.
