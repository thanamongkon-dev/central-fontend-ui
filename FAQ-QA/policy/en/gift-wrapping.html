GIFT WRAPPING

TERMS AND CONDITIONS

  • Free gift-wrapping service is eligible for any purchase with a minimum spending of 200 THB per item.
  • The gift-wrapping service applies to all products sold by Central, any product with an ID starting with CDS and MJT (excluding an item sold by third-party or marketplace sellers).
  • Eligible products for gift wrapping must have the following dimensions and not exceed 38x43x33 cm. with a maximum weight allowance of 5 kg.
  • Purchased items related to a single order may be partially divided into multiple packages for gift wrapping and delivered according to each operational store.
  • The limit is 1 gift message per order, as stated during the checkout process. This message will be attached to a gift box.
