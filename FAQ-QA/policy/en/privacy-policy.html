7. Cookies and How We Use Cookies

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information from you through the use of cookies. Cookies are small pieces of data or text issued to your computer when you visit a website and are used to store or track information about your website usage, analyze trends, manage the website, track user movements, or remember user preferences. Some types of cookies are essential for the website to function properly, while others allow us to enhance your browsing experience, customize content to your needs, and make your interactions with the website more convenient by remembering your username (in a secure way) and language preferences. Most internet browsers allow you to decide whether to accept cookies. If you refuse, delete, or block cookies, your user experience may be affected, and without cookies, your ability to use all or parts of the website may be limited. In addition, third parties may issue cookies through our website to deliver advertisements relevant to your interests based on your browsing activities. These third parties may collect your browsing history or other information to understand how you access the website and which pages you visit after leaving our site. Information collected through these automatic means may be linked to personal data you have previously provided on our website.

You may have the right to request the restriction of the use of your personal data in certain cases.

7) Erasure or Destruction:

You may have the right to request that we erase, destroy, or anonymize your personal data that we collect, use, or disclose, unless we are required to retain such data for legal compliance, to establish legal claims, for use, or to protect legal claims.
