Step to Report a Fake Page

In case you suffer damages from other channels impersonating Central Online.

  • Bring the documents or evidence of the damages to file a police report.
  • Contact the Call Center at 1425, press 2, Monday to Sunday from 09:00 AM to 09:00 PM
  • Attach the documents via email : contact@central.co.th Monday to Sunday from 09:00 AM to 09:00 PM as follows
  • Police report document
  • Evidence of the money transfer or the damages incurred
  • Information of the complainant or the victim
  • The page name and the URL of that page

Note: After receiving the complaint information, your case will be forwarded to the legal department for further action.

In case no damages are found from the impersonation of Central Online.

  • Report the details of that page
  • Contact the Call Center at 1425, press 2, Monday to Sunday from 09:00 AM to 09:00 PM

To determine whether the page is an official Central page, we recommend you verify it.
